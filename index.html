About Me

Hello! My name is Alonso De La Torre Vega. I'm an Informatics graduate from the University of California, Irvine, with a specialization in Organizations and Information Technology. That said, however, my main interest is Human Computer Interaction. I want to be a part of technology research and design, to help bridge the gap between social and technical perspectives. In the end, both technology and people will continually exist so it's only right we try to smoothly incorporate the two.

Given that, as an aspiring UX/UI designer, I strive to make my design decisions based on human-centered processes. I believe technological designs should be based on making technology work for people. Change is inevitable and through these processes, we can assure the implementation of well-designed technology.

When I'm not thinking about UX/UI design, I'm at the movies. I mostly like to watch new movies, but really as long as I'm at the theatre I'm happy. When it comes to food, I won't turn anything down! Occasionally, I will search for new local restaurants to try; for the most part, they have been successful attempts at new foods I now enjoy. I take opportunities to try new things and be adventurous. When I'm not outdoors I'm practicing my design skills, specifically with digital design apps like Sketch and Axure. I feel the need to expand my knowledge of different tools and resources available to me, in order to have a diverse toolset.
