About Me



Hello! My name is Alonso De La Torre Vega. I'm a senior studying Informatics
at the University of California, Irvine. My specialization is Organizations and
Information Technology, however I'm much more intrigued with the human
aspect of technology. 

That said, as an aspiring UX/UI designer, I strive to make my design decisions
based on human-centered processes. I believe technological designs should be
based on making technology work for people.

During my free time, I like to hang with friends and be adventurous. I enjoy taking
the opportunity to try new things and be outdoors. When I'm not outdoors, I'm 
learning new design tools and experimenting with them. It's nice to brush up on old
tools, but it's even nicer to learn new ones!
